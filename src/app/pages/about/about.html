Our Story

A community of faith, hope, and love in Irenipuram.

History & Heritage

Founded on Faith,
Growing in Grace

Living Light Christian Assembly began with a small group of believers gathering to seek God's face. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant family dedicated to serving Irenipuram and beyond.

We believe that the church is not just a building, but a people—called to love God and love our neighbors. Whether you've been here for years or are visiting for the first time, you are welcome home.

Our Core Values
